Thailand — Phang Nga, Phi Phi & Krabi

Home › Destinations › Thailand — Phang Nga, Phi Phi & Krabi
The trip that started it all. In April 2012 we packed our bags and flew to Thailand with Noah, who was just 1½ years old, and Line pregnant with Vitus. We had no grand plan — just beach bungalows, longtail boats, and a desire to see if travelling with a baby was even possible. We explored Phang Nga’s stunning coastline, took a day trip to the famous Maya Bay on Phi Phi, survived a real tsunami warning on Koh Lanta, and finished at the breathtaking Railay Beach in Krabi. It was chaotic, exhausting, beautiful, and it changed everything. Eight months later we were back — this time with two kids. Blog posts from this trip below.
