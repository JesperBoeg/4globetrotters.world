How We Chose Snorkeling Days

  • Short boat windows over all-day marathons.
  • Conditions first: visibility, current, and boat crowding.
  • Warm-up swims before trying deeper or busier sites.

Safety and Comfort Rules

  • One adult fully focused on child supervision.
  • Frequent breaks and hydration in shade.
  • No pressure to "complete" every stop if conditions change.
