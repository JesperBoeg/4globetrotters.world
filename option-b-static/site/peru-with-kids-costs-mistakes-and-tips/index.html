Peru With Kids: Costs, Mistakes, and Tips

What we spent, what we misjudged, and what we would repeat

Home › Peru With Kids Costs Guide

Cost Reality (Family Perspective)

  • Big-ticket items are usually guided treks and multi-stop tours.
  • Local meals can still be great value away from top tourist strips.
  • Transfer-heavy routes look cheap on paper but add up fast.

Mistakes We Made

  • Too many hard days in a row around altitude-sensitive locations.
  • Underestimating recovery time after long overland transport.
  • Accepting tight day-tour pacing that looked better in brochures than in reality.

What We Would Do Again

  • Buffer days before major hikes.
  • Mix one high-effort day with one light day.
  • Keep kids involved by choosing one key highlight per location.
