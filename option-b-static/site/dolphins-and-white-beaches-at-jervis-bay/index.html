Dolphins and white beaches at Jervis Bay

By LineogJesper on February 8, 2016

After saying goodbye to our good friends from Denmark (and the boys telling us how much they were already missing their friends Anton and Emil 12 times every hour ) we headed to Jervis bay. Jervis bay is only 2-3 hours south of Sydney but a different world all together. With extremely white beaches (the worlds whitest according to the Guinness Book of Records), clean and crystal clear water and a pack of dolphins numbering roughly 80, it is truly a gem.
