Hej, we are the 4Globetrotters

We are a family of four from Denmark: Jesper, Line, Noah and Vitus. We have always loved traveling, but once we became parents the dream changed from quick holidays to long, meaningful adventures together.

In 2015 we took the leap and left for a 6.5-month round-the-world journey. That trip changed everything. Since then, we have kept exploring as a family and documenting both the highlights and the messy real-life moments on this blog.

What started as a way to update family and friends has grown into a practical travel diary and a source of inspiration for other families who want to see more of the world with their children.

Our Travel Story

2015-2016: First Round-the-World Trip

Cyprus, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines, Australia, Hawaii, California, Mexico, Cuba and the Deep South in the USA.

2017: Back on the Road

Northern Territory and Queensland in Australia, then Bali and nearby islands in Indonesia.

2019: US National Parks Dream

Rocky Mountains, Yellowstone, Bryce, Zion, Antelope Canyon and Grand Canyon.

2022: Pacific Chapter

San Francisco, French Polynesia, Fiji and Western Australia, with Singapore on the way home.

2024: South America

Galapagos, mainland Ecuador and Peru, including Amazon wildlife and high-altitude trekking.

Today

Still exploring, still learning, and still sharing practical experiences for family travel.
