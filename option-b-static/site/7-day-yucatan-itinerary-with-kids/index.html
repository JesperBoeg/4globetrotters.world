7-Day Yucatan Itinerary With Kids

A realistic family route east to west: Riviera Maya → Valladolid → Merida

Home › Yucatan 7-Day Family Itinerary

Itinerary Overview

  • Day 1: Fly into Cancun → base in Puerto Morelos or Tulum (rest day, local food)
  • Day 2: Coba ruins + cenotes day trip (Choo-Ha, Tamcach-Ha, Multum-Ha nearby)
  • Day 3: Sian Ka’an or Akumal beach (sea turtles, rays, snorkeling)
  • Day 4: Transfer to Valladolid + afternoon at Chichen Itza (go early to beat heat)
  • Day 5: Valladolid slower day — Ria Lagartos flamingo reserve if energy allows
  • Day 6: Transfer to Merida, settle in
  • Day 7: Cuzama cenotes or Uxmal + Grutas de Calcehtok

Family Pacing Rules

  • One main activity per day is enough — two ruins in a row break small kids.
  • Start Chichen Itza before 9am to get ahead of tour buses and heat.
  • Valladolid makes a better base than Cancun for the interior — we found our best AirBnB of the entire trip there.
  • Pair every hard-sun day with a cenote or pool afternoon.

Budget Notes

  • Car rental gives flexibility and often lowers day-trip costs.
  • Food outside top tourist streets is much better value.
  • Some ruins add multiple ticket components; verify full price in advance.
