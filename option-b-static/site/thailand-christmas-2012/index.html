Thailand — Christmas in Krabi

Home › Destinations › Thailand — Christmas in Krabi
Eight months after our first trip to Thailand, we went back. This time with two kids — Noah was 2½ and Vitus was just 3 months old. We spent Christmas 2012 on the Krabi coast, and it was a completely different experience from our first trip. With a newborn and a toddler, everything takes longer, weighs more, and requires roughly triple the planning. But we still managed elephant riding (which we’d view very differently today), found the stunning Railay Beach, watched ridiculous sunsets, and had a Christmas we’ll never forget. This was our second trip and it cemented the idea that travelling with small children wasn’t just possible — it was how we wanted to live. Blog posts from this trip below.
